Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between the Group and Premium offerings?

The Group Offering provides comprehensive training in a community setting with group coaching. The Premium Offering includes everything in the Group Offering plus four private 1:1 coaching sessions, priority support, and exclusive advanced resources for a more personalized experience.

How are payments processed?

All payments are securely processed through Stripe. You can choose to pay in full or select one of our payment plans. For payment plans, your card will be automatically charged on the same day each month.

When does the course start?

The next cohort begins on June 9, 2025. Once you complete your payment, you'll receive a welcome email with instructions on how to access the course materials and join the first live session.

How do the payment plans work?

For the 3-month payment plan, your card will be charged the first payment at checkout, with subsequent payments 30 and 60 days later. For the 6-month plan, your card will be charged the first payment at checkout, with five additional monthly payments. All plans provide immediate full access to the course.
